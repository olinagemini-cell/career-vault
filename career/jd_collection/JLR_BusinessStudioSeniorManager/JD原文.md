## What to Expect

The Business / Studio Senior Manager will play a critical operational support role within the newly established Freelander Design Studio in Shanghai. Working directly with the Freelander Design Director, the role ensures the efficient running of two studio locations, alignment with programme management teams, and seamless collaboration between China and UK based stakeholders.

This manager will be instrumental in supporting governance, financial control, supplier management, and cross-functional communication across the JLR–Chery joint venture ecosystem—ultimately enabling the creative team to deliver world-class Freelander design.

## Key Responsibilities

### Studio Operations & Leadership
- Oversee day-to-day operational management of two Shanghai design studio sites, ensuring they operate efficiently, safely, and within all governance frameworks.
- Manage staff resourcing and agency personnel coordination, working closely with programme management teams and HR to ensure correct, timely onboarding and contract oversight.
- Lead local business processes, including workspace planning, studio utilisation, policy adherence, and operational systems.

### Business Support to the Design Director
- Provide high-quality preparation and coordination for all senior-level engagements: JLR Board updates, JV (Chery–JLR) executive meetings, Programme review sessions, governance forums and design reviews.
- Support the Director with pastoral responsibilities: team communications, development planning, onboarding, and engagement initiatives.

### Financial, Procurement & Supplier Management
- Oversee local budgeting, supplier relations, procurement cycles, purchase orders, and financial forecasts for the Shanghai studio.
- Ensure tight financial alignment with the UK Creative Business Office, supporting accurate reporting and submission of budget requirements.
- Manage local and international supplier onboarding, contract compliance, and performance monitoring.

### Cross-Functional & International Collaboration
- Act as a key operational link between Shanghai Design Studio, JLRC Exec team, UK Creative Business Office, UK Creative leadership, UK Freelander MD's Team, Chery Freelander CEO, and Chery Freelander Engineering counterparts.
- Ensure adherence to all Freelander governance processes, reporting cycles, and JV-specific requirements.
- Drive alignment between UK–China teams, enabling smooth delivery of programme milestones and cross-regional decision-making.

### Programme & IP Management
- Support Director and programme leads in operational governance of the Freelander brand design programme: risk, issue, dependency tracking.
- Manage all design-related procurement, ensuring compliance with both JLR and JV requirements.
- Oversee IP governance: proper documentation, storage, and protection of design assets.

## Knowledge, Skills, Experience

### Essential
- Proven experience in studio management, business operations, or programme/business support within design, automotive, creative, or technology environments.
- Strong capability in multi-site operational management.
- Excellent communication skills with demonstrated ability to support senior leadership.
- Strong organisational and time-management skills, coordinating complex, multi-stakeholder tasks.
- Financial literacy: budgeting, forecasting, procurement processes.
- Cross-cultural experience; ability to bridge teams across geographies and organisations.

### Desirable
- Experience within automotive OEMs, creative studios, or joint venture environments.
- Understanding of design processes, design governance, and product development lifecycles.
- Mandarin/English bilingual proficiency.
- Prior experience working with UK-based headquarters or global matrix organisations.

> Closing line: An individual with the ability to challenge existing thinking in a positive way whilst building credibility and trust through experience and personal style.
